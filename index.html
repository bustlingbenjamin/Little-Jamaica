About Us

We are conviently located in the vibrant area of Killeen, Little Jamaica Restaurant & Lounge sits on the busy street of Willow Springs Rd, a close proximity to Fort Hood the largest military base, minutes from the city buildings, and surrounded by a diverse community and hotels. Little Jamaica Restaurant and Lounge is committed to serving fresh seafood, offering live entertainment daily, and creating memorable moments nightly for our guests.

Enjoy Our Food Experience

We don’t just make food. We make people’s experience. Little Jamaica was built on the belief that food should be special, and we carry that belief into everything we do.
